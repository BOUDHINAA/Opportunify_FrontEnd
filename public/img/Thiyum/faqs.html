Frequently Asked Questions

WHAT IS THYIM?

THYIM is Everyday people helping everyday people in 15 minutes or less.

HOW DO YOU PRONOUNCE THYIM?

THYIM is pronounced like ‘them.’

WHAT DOES THYIM DO?

We provide everyday services in 15 minutes or less.

WHAT DOES THYIM MEAN?

They Help You In Minutes.

WHAT DOES THE ACRONYM THYIM STAND FOR?

They Help You In Minutes.

HOW DO I BECOME A HELPER?

Sign-up on our website: www.thyim.com or download our THYIM app (available for android and iOS). Must be 18 years or older to apply and must pass a background check.

WHAT IS A HELPER?

A Helper is a person that provides services to PINS (People in Need of Services) arriving in 15 minutes or less.

WHAT IS A PINS?

PINS are People in Need of Service.

DO PINS NEED TO CREATE AN ACCOUNT?

PINS are not required to create an account to receive service.

DO HELPERS NEED TO CREATE AN ACCOUNT?

Helpers must create an account in order to work for the company.

DOES IT COST TO BECOME A HELPER?

No. There is no cost to become a Helper.

WHAT SERVICES DOES THYIM OFFER?

We offer a wide range of services for Indoor, Outdoor, and Roadside. Please visit our website or download our app for the most updated information.

WHAT ARE THE HOURS OF OPERATION AT THYIM?

24/7

ARE HELPERS BACKGROUND CHECKED?

Yes. All Helpers are given a thorough background check before working for our company.

HOW DO I CONTACT CUSTOMER SERVICE?

1-833-THYIM15 or 1-833-849-4615

CAN I DELETE MY THYIM ACCOUNT?

Yes. You can delete both a THYIM PIN account and a THYIM Helper account.
The easiest way to do this, is to go to either App, go to the menu, and at the bottom of the menu - in red text- simply click on the Delete Account section and follow the steps.
Alternatively you can request to delete your account by contacting THYIM Customer Service at:
1-833-THYIM15 or 1-833-849-4615

CAN I DELETE MY PERSONAL DATA?

Yes you can. To delete your personal data, please contact THYIM Customer Service at: 1-833-THYIM15 or 1-833-849-4615

HOW MUCH ARE THE SERVICES AT THYIM?

Prices vary depending on services selected.

HOW DO I CONTACT THYIM?

Helpers and PINS can email hapi@thyim.com or call us at 1-833-THYIM15 or 1-833-849-4615 For any issues with our mobile applications or website, please email techsupport@thyim.com For all other inquiries, please email us at admin@thyim.com
