THYIM is a new company founded and based on what we all have in common, a true need for help.

THYIM is a new company founded and based on what we all have in common, a true need for help. The concept first came to the Founder over 5 years ago, after being stranded on Interstate 96 in Novi, Michigan. R.M. contacted a roadside assistance provider and was told service would arrive within 60 minutes. This simply was not the case. After waiting over four hours in the middle of winter, their vehicle out of fuel, R.M. thought, “I just wish somebody, anybody, could bring me some gas.”

In August 2022, R.M. spoke to a 92-year-old woman sitting on the side of the road waiting for a major roadside service company to assist her. There was a huge delay which resulted in the elderly woman soiling her garments and feeling too embarrassed to ask for further assistance. R.M. was instantly taken back to that experience on the highway of not having access to food, water, or a bathroom.

These situations were the catalyst to get THYIM up and running and to ensure people receive help safely and in an adequate amount if time.
